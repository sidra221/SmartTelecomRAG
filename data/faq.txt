Q: How can I activate a new SIM card?
A: To activate a new SIM card, insert it into your phone and restart the device. Activation usually completes within a few minutes. If activation does not occur automatically, contact customer support.

Q: How can I check my remaining balance?
A: You can check your remaining balance by dialing *123# or through the official telecom mobile application.

Q: What should I do if my internet connection is slow?
A: If your internet connection is slow, try restarting your router or mobile device. Make sure you are within network coverage. If the issue persists, contact customer support.

Q: How can I contact customer service?
A: You can contact customer service by calling 111, sending an email, or using the live chat feature on our website.

Q: What happens if I do not pay my bill on time?
A: If the bill is not paid on time, outgoing services may be temporarily suspended until the payment is completed.
